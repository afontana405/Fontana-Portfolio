About Alex Fontana

Born October 8th, 2001 in Atlanta, Georgia. By the time Alex Fontana graduated highschool, he had called 5 states and 8 different cities his home. After 2 years in college, I realized this path was not for me. While taking a gap year to figure out what was next for me, I came upon a coding bootcamp being offered by the University of Minnesota, where I am currently enrolled. That is where I have learned the basics of coding and developed the skills to make this portfolio as well as the other projects being shown off down below. While coding can be frustrating at times, the feeling of completing a project you have spents hours on remains unmatched.
